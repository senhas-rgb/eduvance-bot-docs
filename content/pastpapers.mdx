# 9. Educational Resources (Edexcel Past Papers)

Search and access Edexcel past papers directly from Discord.

---

## 9.1 `/pastpaper`

Search Edexcel past papers by subject, year, and series, returning direct links.

```discord filename="Discord" copy
/pastpaper <subject> <year> <exam_series>
```

> [!NOTE]
>
> This command is available to all members.

#### Parameters

- `subject` – e.g. Accounting, Biology, Business, Chemistry, Economics, English Language, IT, Mathematics, Physics, Psychology, etc.
- `year` – typically 2019–2025
- `exam_series` – January, June, or October

#### Behavior

- Validates parameters, builds a query, fetches results
- Shows a formatted embed with direct links to past papers

> [!TIP]
>
> Promote this command in student channels so members can quickly fetch Edexcel papers for revision.

---

### Supported Subjects

The bot supports a wide range of Edexcel subjects including:

- Accounting
- Biology
- Business
- Chemistry
- Economics
- English Language
- Information Technology (IT)
- Mathematics
- Physics
- Psychology

And many more. Use the command's autocomplete feature to see all available subjects.

---

### Exam Series

Edexcel exams are typically held in three series per year:

- **January** – Winter series
- **June** – Summer series
- **October** – Autumn series

> [!NOTE]
>
> Not all subjects are available in all series. The bot will show available papers for your selected parameters.
